Step #2 - Status: IN PROGRESS

Each week, you will receive a postcard with an encrypted clue. Once decrypted, each clue replaces one of the empty spaces in the sentence to the right.

Click this button to download a copy of the sentence to work offline.

Once complete, the sentence will give you a location on Google Maps where you will find a number.

Using the unique Investigator ID number that was given to you in the original letter, send our CEO an email with that number and your unique ID in the subjet matter of the email.

If the number is correct, you will be formally hired as one of our Second Sparks and you will receive a badge and a contract.
If the number is incorrect, don't worry: being a good investigator means you need to be able to get it wrong, backtrack and try again so you can send your answer more than once!
