Step #2 - Status: IN PROGRESS

Each week, you will receive a postcard with an encrypted clue. Once decrypted, each clue replaces one of the empty spaces in the sentence on the black board.

Once complete, the sentence will give you a location on Google Maps where you will find a number.

Send our CEO an email with the subject section containing the number you found and the unique ID you were given in the first letter you received.

If the number is correct, you will be formally hired as one of our Second Sparks and you will receive a badge and a contract.

If the number is incorrect, don't worry: a good investigator knows they will get it wrong sometimes and are willing to backtrack and try again. You can send your answer more than once!
